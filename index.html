About Us

Always committed to the highest standards in food and qualty service

Welcome to Tacos Lupita! We’re delighted to have you visit our website and learn more about our family business. Our journey began on November 1st, 1999, in the heart of Somerville, MA. Since day one, we've dedicated ourselves to providing exceptional service and mouth-watering food, which has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our passion and hard work led us to expand, and on July 15th, 2004, we proudly opened our second restaurant in Lawrence, MA. The love and support from our community have been incredible, allowing us to grow further with new locations in Haverhill, MA on July 15th, 2005, and in Lowell, MA on November 22nd, 2015.

At Tacos Lupita, we recognize that our success is deeply tied to our wonderful customers. That’s why we are committed to upholding the highest standards in food quality and customer service. Each meal we serve is a testament to our dedication to you.

We can't wait to welcome you to any of our restaurants, where you’ll always find a warm atmosphere and delicious food waiting for you.

With the warmest regards,

Leo & Elsa Zúñiga
